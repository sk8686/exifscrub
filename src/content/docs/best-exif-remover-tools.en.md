There are many EXIF remover tools available, but they vary significantly in privacy, ease of use, and effectiveness.

## Online EXIF Removers

### Our Tool (EXIF Remover)
- Processing: 100% local (browser-based)
- Privacy: Files never leave your device
- Formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, HEIC
- Batch support: Yes
- Cost: Free

**Best for**: Anyone who wants a fast, private, no-compromise EXIF removal experience.

### ExifData.io
- Processing: 100% local (browser-based)
- Privacy: Files never leave your device
- Formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, TIFF, HEIC, PDF, DOCX
- Batch support: Yes
- Cost: Free

**Best for**: Users who also need to clean PDF and DOCX metadata.

**Limitations**: No before/after comparison. HEIC converted to PNG (quality loss).

### ExifRemover.com
- Processing: 100% local (WebAssembly)
- Privacy: Files never leave your device
- Formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, HEIC, GIF, AVIF, MP4, MOV, PDF
- Batch support: Yes (up to 20 files)
- Cost: Free

**Best for**: Users who need video format support.

**Limitations**: No before/after comparison. Video metadata can only be detected, not removed.

### VerExif.com
- Processing: Server-side (uploads your photos)
- Privacy: ⚠️ Photos are uploaded to their server
- Formats: JPG only
- Batch support: No
- Cost: Free

**Not recommended** due to privacy concerns — uploading photos to a server contradicts the purpose of a privacy tool.

## Desktop Software

### ExifTool (Command Line)
- Fully private, supports virtually all formats, free and open source
- **Best for**: Power users and automation scripts
- **Limitations**: Command-line only, not user-friendly

### ExifCleaner (Desktop App)
- Fully private, supports many formats, free and open source
- **Best for**: Users who prefer a desktop application
- **Limitations**: Requires download and installation

## Recommendation

For most users, a browser-based tool that processes files locally is the best choice. It requires no installation, works on any device, and doesn't require uploading your photos to a server.
